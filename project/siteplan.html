Flavors of Mexico

Site plan for a traditional Mexican food website featuring classic dishes, history, and ingredients.

Site Name

Flavors of Mexico

The name highlights Mexican culinary heritage and invites visitors to explore traditional flavors.

Suggested domain: flavorsofmexico.org

Site Purpose

The site will introduce traditional Mexican dishes such as tacos, tamales, mole, and pozole with short histories, common ingredients, photos, and cultural facts.

Scenarios

  • What are the key ingredients and origins of mole and pozole?
  • Where can I find quick facts and photos about iconic Mexican dishes?

Color Schema

Agave Green #0B3D2E

Used for headings and primary highlights.

Flag Red #8B1E1E

Used for accents, buttons, and key callouts.

Agave Cream #F7F1E3

Primary background and content panels.

Typography

Merriweather Bold — For headings and primary sections.

Inter Regular/Semibold — For body text, labels, and navigation.
